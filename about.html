past

With sore thumbs and a desire to breathe life into my own ideas, I started my journey to turn my love of video games into a career very early on. Starting with a TRS-80 and learning by copying code snippets out of magazines, I gradually worked my way up to better hardware and code of my own. After spending some time developing games in more traditional programming languages, such as C++, I found new love in Adobe Flash. The ability to rapidly bring my ideas to fruition and have them playable by everyone across the web made Flash/Actionscript my goto tool. For the past 11 years, I have had a great career creating games spanning all genres and working at companies such as Acclaim, Kabam and Disney.

present

My focus has now shifted over to mobile/desktop development primarily in Unity. I'm also wiping the cobwebs off of my old C++ skills to get back to my roots. Keep an eye on this site and my github page to see my upcoming game releases, side projects and experiments.

future

If it is not evident yet, let me make it clear that I love to build, create and learn! Expect to see articles and experiments on the latest thing that I'm playing with posted here. I'm excited to see what the future brings and I hope my musing and tinkerings on this site will help others.
